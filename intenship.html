About the NGO

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization working for social welfare and community development. The foundation supports underprivileged communities through education, food support, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental protection and skill development.


Our mission is to create a society where every person gets opportunities, care and support. Through different projects, InAmigos Foundation encourages people to join hands for positive change.
